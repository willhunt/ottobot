# Motors

Dagu Wild Thumper Motors [Product Page]

The wild thumper was bought with 34:1 motors and swapped out with 75:1 motors as this was the cheaper option at the time.

Motor Max Speed [rpm] Voltage [V] Stall Current [A] Stall Torque [Kg.cm]
75:1 133 6 (7.5 max) 3.4 12
34:1 133 6 (7.5 max) 3.4 12

The cable supplied with the motors is 20AWG.

# Changing the gearbox

The gearboxes for motors with a 75:1 and 34:1 gear ratio can be swapped over.

Installed motor
Remove screws
Gently remove front & mid gearbox
Take donor motor
Remove gearbox and fit
Note slightly different sizes

Notes:

  • The 75:1 ratio gearbox is slightly longer
  • The 75:1 ratio gearbox used longer screws
